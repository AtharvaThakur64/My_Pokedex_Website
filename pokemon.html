Pokémon Types

Normal Type

Normal-type Pokémon are generally well-rounded, but they are not super effective against any other type. They are weak against Fighting types.

Fire Type

Fire-type Pokémon are strong and fiery, excelling in hot environments. They are strong against Grass, Ice, Bug, and Steel types but weak against Water, Rock, and Fire types.

Electric Type

Electric-type Pokémon are fast and efficient, often striking quickly. They are strong against Water and Flying types but weak against Ground types.

Grass Type

Grass-type Pokémon are known for their regenerative abilities and connection to nature. They are strong against Water, Ground, and Rock types but weak against Fire, Ice, Poison, Flying, and Bug types.

Ice Type

Ice-type Pokémon thrive in cold environments and can freeze their foes. They are strong against Grass, Ground, Flying, and Dragon types but weak against Fire, Fighting, Rock, and Steel types.

Fighting Type

Fighting-type Pokémon are powerful and excel in physical combat. They are strong against Normal, Ice, Rock, Dark, and Steel types but weak against Flying, Psychic, and Fairy types.

Poison Type

Poison-type Pokémon use toxic attacks to wear down their opponents. They are strong against Grass and Fairy types but weak against Ground and Psychic types.

Ground Type

Ground-type Pokémon are resilient and can create powerful tremors. They are strong against Fire, Electric, Poison, Rock, and Steel types but weak against Water, Ice, and Grass types.

Flying Type

Flying-type Pokémon are agile and excel in aerial battles. They are strong against Grass, Fighting, and Bug types but weak against Electric, Ice, and Rock types.

Psychic Type

Psychic-type Pokémon have powerful mental abilities. They are strong against Fighting and Poison types but weak against Bug, Ghost, and Dark types.

Bug Type

Bug-type Pokémon are often small but can be very effective in battles. They are strong against Grass, Psychic, and Dark types but weak against Fire, Flying, and Rock types.

Rock Type

Rock-type Pokémon are durable and can withstand many attacks. They are strong against Fire, Ice, Flying, and Bug types but weak against Water, Grass, Fighting, Ground, and Steel types.

Ghost Type

Ghost-type Pokémon are mysterious and often have abilities related to the supernatural. They are strong against Psychic and Ghost types but weak against Ghost and Dark types.

Dragon Type

Dragon-type Pokémon are powerful and often have legendary status. They are strong against Dragon types but weak against Ice, Dragon, and Fairy types.

Dark Type

Dark-type Pokémon use underhanded tactics and excel in battles. They are strong against Psychic and Ghost types but weak against Fighting, Bug, and Fairy types.

Steel Type

Steel-type Pokémon are incredibly resilient and often have metallic bodies. They are strong against Ice, Rock, and Fairy types but weak against Fire, Fighting, and Ground types.

Fairy Type

Fairy-type Pokémon are enchanting and often have magical abilities. They are strong against Fighting, Dragon, and Dark types but weak against Poison and Steel types.
